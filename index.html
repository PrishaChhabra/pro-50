PIGEON

Fun Facts about Pigeon

  • They are incredibly complex and inteligent animals.
  • They can find their way back to the nest from 1300 miles away.
  • A study suggests that, given the right conditions, they're as good at identifying cancer as doctors.

PANDA

Fun Facts about Panda

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and swimming too.
  • An adult can eat 12-38 kilos of bamboo per day.
  • They enjoy licking scraps of food from metallic bowls and have particular affinity towards copper and iron.

CHAMELEON

Fun Facts about Chameleon

  • They have eyes at the back of their heads.
  • Chameleons mainly change color in order to communicate or regulate body temperature.
  • Unlike many lizards, they cannot re-grow their tails.

TEDDY-BEAR

Fun Facts about Teddy-Bear

  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term back to 1906.
  • The world's smallest teddy is 0.29 inches tall, crafted by a South African miniature artist.
  • The world's largest teddy is 5-feet-4-inches tall. It is kept in Kansas, United States.

FLYING-CICADA

Fun Facts about Flying-Cicada

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • They are about the size of a grain of rice when they drop from a tree branch to the ground and start digging.
  • Cicadas don’t sing at night, so don’t blame them if you can’t sleep.
